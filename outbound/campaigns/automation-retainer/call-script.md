# Cold Call Script — Automation Retainer (your day-8 cash lever)

**Why calls:** $0 cost, works today, no warmup. Cap ~50–75/week. Call during business hours (9am–5pm local). Goal of the call = **book a demo OR close on the spot.** Have the demo site open.

**Mindset:** you're not begging — you're bringing a roofer a way to stop losing $8k jobs. You're doing them a favor. Smile, slow down, be human.

---

## The opener (first 10 seconds decide everything)

> "Hey, is this [Name]? ... Hey [Name], this is [Your Name] from Xevaro Labs — I'll be honest, this is a cold call, but it's a quick one. Can I take 30 seconds and you tell me if it's worth continuing?"

(The honesty + permission disarms them. Almost everyone says "go ahead.")

---

## The problem (make them feel it)

> "So — when you're up on a roof or out on a job and a customer calls, you obviously can't always grab it, right? ... Right. The thing is, that customer doesn't leave a voicemail and wait. They just call the next roofer on Google. So you're basically losing jobs you never even knew called."

(Pause. Let them agree. They will.)

---

## The pitch (15 seconds)

> "What I do is set up a system that automatically texts back every missed call within about 5 seconds — 'Hey sorry we missed you, this is [Biz], what do you need?' — so the lead stays with YOU instead of going to a competitor. Plus it answers questions and books jobs even after hours."

---

## The close (book or close)

> "I actually built a quick demo for [Business]. Do you have 15 minutes later today or tomorrow, or honestly — I can just text you the demo link right now and you can see it in 2 minutes. Which is easier?"

If engaged and asking about price → close:
> "It's $1,000 a month, plus a $300 setup. Here's the way to think about it: one job you save pays for it for the better part of a year. Want me to get you set up this week?"

---

## Objection handling

| They say | You say |
|---|---|
| "I'm busy / on a job" | "Totally — that's literally the problem I solve. 60 seconds: can I text you the demo and call you tomorrow at this time?" |
| "How much?" | "$1k/month, $300 setup. But honestly the number doesn't matter if it's saving you $8k jobs — let me show you how it works first." |
| "I already have a website / answering service" | "Nice — does it text the customer back automatically when you miss a call? ... That's the gap. That's all this is." |
| "Send me something" | "Done — texting you the demo right now. I'll follow up Thursday. Sound good?" (then ACTUALLY follow up) |
| "Not interested" | "No worries. Quick one before I go — how are you handling missed calls today?" (often re-opens) |
