# Offer: "Never Miss a Lead" — Automation Retainer for Local Trades

**Who it's for:** roofers, plumbers, HVAC, electricians, junk removal, fencing, tree service — trades that run jobs all day and can't answer every call.

**The core insight you sell on:** when these guys are on a roof or under a sink, they miss calls. A missed call = the customer dials the next business on Google. That's a $5K–15K job lost in 20 seconds. They feel this pain daily and have no system for it.

---

## What's included

| Component | What it does | Their pain it kills |
|---|---|---|
| **Missed-call text-back** | Any missed call auto-gets an SMS in ~5 sec: *"Sorry we missed you — this is [Biz]. What can we help with?"* | Lost jobs to voicemail |
| **Speed-to-lead reply** | Web form / Google lead gets an instant text + email | Slow follow-up losing leads |
| **AI responder (24/7)** | Answers FAQs (hours, pricing, service area), qualifies, books | Can't answer after hours |
| **Rebuilt website** | Fast, mobile, click-to-call "front door" | Old site that loses trust |
| **Monthly report** | Leads captured + jobs booked by the system | Proof it's working → retention |
